# Business Recommendations

Short, actionable items extracted from the full analysis in `docs/business-insights.md`.

---

## Immediate (This Month)

1. **Win back 7 At-Risk customers** — send a personalised 10% discount on their most-purchased category
2. **Cap Electronics discounts at 5%** — 10–15% discounts on ₹45K laptops are unnecessarily cutting into the best-revenue product
3. **Identify and acknowledge top 43 Champions** — a loyalty email with no discount required; recognition alone improves retention

## Short-Term (Next Quarter)

4. **Plan a Diwali campaign** for October–November — gift bundles across Laptop, Smartphone, Saree, Jacket; target 25% revenue uplift in Q4
5. **Test Electronics ads in Jaipur and Lucknow** — these cities are underperforming relative to income levels; free delivery is the most effective hook
6. **Bundle Laptop with Keyboard and Headphones** — increases AOV and reduces single-SKU revenue concentration risk

## Medium-Term (Next 6 Months)

7. **Expand Fashion catalog by 4–6 SKUs** — at 63.8% margin, Fashion is the most efficient category to grow
8. **Reduce Grocery SKU count** — Rice, Cooking Oil, and Detergent contribute minimal revenue at the lowest margin (32.4%); consider removing or replacing with higher-margin alternatives
9. **Build a cohort retention report** — identify at which order number customers typically churn and design an intervention at that point
