# Business Recommendations

## Revenue Growth
- Laptop and Smartphone account for 58% of total revenue. Prioritize in promotions, bundles, and inventory.
- Replicate seasonal boosts (Oct-Dec Electronics, Jun/Nov-Dec Fashion) with planned campaigns.
- Cross-sell accessories alongside high-ticket items to raise basket size.

## Customer Value
- Create VIP programs for Champions and Loyal Customers.
- Win-back campaigns for At Risk customers at 30-60 days inactivity.
- Move Potential Loyalists into repeat-purchase journeys.

## Profitability
- Fashion (63.7%) and Stationery (62.8%) have highest margins - grow these.
- Electronics margin is only 38.8% despite leading revenue - reduce blanket discounting.
- Track product-level margin monthly.

## Forecasting
- 3-month rolling average shows steady upward growth 2024-2025.
- Extend with seasonal decomposition in Power BI or Python statsmodels.

## Retention
- Launch 7-day and 30-day post-purchase email sequences - cohort drop-off highest in month 1.
- Use RFM segments to personalize re-engagement rather than batch campaigns.
- Target >40% 3-month retention for new customer cohorts.
